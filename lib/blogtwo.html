FUll Stack 101

January, 2023

What is fullstack? The term “full stack” refers to the technologies and skills needed to complete a project, with each individual component being a stack. Stacks can be mobile, Web or software specific. Typically, a software engineer will focus on one part of development, either the front end or the back end.
